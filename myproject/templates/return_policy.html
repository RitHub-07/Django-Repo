How to Return

Start Your Return Online

Go to "My Orders" and select the item you want to return. Choose a reason and submit your request.

Package Your Item

Include all original packaging, tags, and accessories. Print and attach the return label we provide.

Wait for Pickup

Our delivery partner will collect the package from your doorstep within 2 business days.

Refund Information

Instant Refunds

For payments made via Flippkart Pay Later, credit/debit cards, or UPI, refunds are processed within 24 hours of us receiving your return.

Original Payment Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Store Credit

Opt for Flippkart Credit to get 5% extra on your refund amount (credited instantly).

Processing Times
  • Electronics: 3-5 business days after quality check
  • Fashion: 2-3 business days
  • Large appliances: 5-7 business days
